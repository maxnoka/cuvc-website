Teams

Overview
The club is represented by 4 teams: Blues Women, Blues Men, UCC Women and UCC Men.

The Blues teams comprise of the very best players from across the University. Blues players will be able to represent the University in the Premier division of the British University and College Sports (BUCS) league.

The United Cambridge College (UCC) squads allows players with less experience to still train and play volleyball at an intermediate level. Whle there is more emphasis on player development, the UCC teams also play competitively and represent the Univeristy in the BUCS league.

For information on how to join, see the Prospective Players. page
